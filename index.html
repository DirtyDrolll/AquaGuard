Why Water Conservation Matters

Water is essential to all life on Earth, yet only 3% of the world's water is fresh water, and two-thirds of that is frozen in glaciers or otherwise unavailable for use. As the global population grows and climate change impacts water availability, conservation becomes increasingly critical.

At AquaGuard, we believe that education is the first step toward meaningful change. Our mission is to provide accessible information about water conservation practices that individuals, families, and communities can implement to make a difference.

Did You Know?

Leaky Faucets Waste Water

A single dripping faucet can waste over 3,000 gallons per year - enough for 180 showers!

Shower Savings

Reducing your shower time by just 2 minutes can save up to 1,750 gallons per person annually.

Outdoor Water Use

50-70% of household water is used outdoors, mostly for watering lawns and gardens.
